Castlerea,

County Roscommon

Welcome to Castlerea!

Castlerea is based in County Roscommon in the west of Ireland. Castlerea has a population of 1,873 people.

Castlerea has a number of facilities such as:

Golf Course

Castlerea Golf Course is made up of 8 different holes. It has the river suck running through, which is a major part of some of the holes. It was founded in 1905. The golf club really gives off a warm west of Ireland vibe. Check it out here.

Clonalis House

Clonalis House is a historic house and has history dating back to the O'Conor family, descendants of the Kings of Connacht. It offers tours, Events, Food, Drink and Accommodation. Check it out here.

Walking Trails

Castlerea has a number of walking trails in the area. One of the most used trails in the town would be The Suck Valley Way. This trail takes you through the Demesne Parklands, The Woodlands and ends at Clonalis House. The trail is 6KM long. Check out the trail here.

Swimming Pool

Castlerea Swimming Pool is a heated 33 meter outdoor swimming pool. It opens for the summer months. It is supervised by fully qualified lifegaurds. It had a shallow and deep end to suit everyones needs. It has a slide, changing cubicles and a picnic area. It is located directly beside a playground. Check it out here.
